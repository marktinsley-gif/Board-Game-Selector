Optional: Upload Your Game Collection

To get the most out of this app, you can upload your BoardGameGeek collection. This allows group votes to include your games when deciding what to play.

What you'll need:

  • A BoardGameGeek account
  • Games marked as "Owned" in your BGG collection

How to get your CSV file:

  1. Go to your BGG Collection page
  2. Click on your username at the top
  3. Select "Collection" from the menu
  4. Look for the "Export" option (usually near the filters)
  5. Download the CSV or TSV file

You can always upload your collection later from your home page.
